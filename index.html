Datum Notes
Kertau 1948 Datum: This app uses the Kertau 1948 datum / Modified Everest ellipsoid (EPSG:3168) — same as the official topographic maps of Peninsular Malaysia.

WGS84 Difference: Modern GPS and Google Maps use the WGS84 datum. The difference can reach ~150–200 meters. Do not input GPS coordinates directly without datum conversion.

Coverage: Peninsular Malaysia only — Lat: 1°–7.5°N, Long: 99°–104.5°E.
